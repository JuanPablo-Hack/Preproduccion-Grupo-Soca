Who are we?

We are a freight forwarder that is part of GRUPO SOCA, with a human team with extensive experience in the areas of logistics, customs clearance, sea and air freight.

We efficiently link each area in the foreign trade merchandise supply chain!


Mission

Represent the foreign trade sector, in the chain of global supply, with comprehensive services, for the cost reduction, with efficient logistics.


Vision

Position ourselves as a solution in global logistics.

Services

We are a company with endless services, which are tailored to the needs of our customers, to provide them with the best part of us. Our services are:

  • Storage
  • Crossdock
  • Distribution
  • Local multimodal transport for general, dangerous and oversized cargo (container carrier, chassis, hoppers, low bed, lowboy, dry box, refrigerated boxes)
  • Multimodal foreign transport, for general, dangerous, and oversized cargo (container carrier, chassis, hoppers, low bed, lowboy, dry box, refrigerated boxes)
  • Courtyard for maneuvers
  • Loading and unloading of Full containers
  • Loading and unloading of Empty containers.
  • Container Consolidation and Deconsolidation
  • Counting and separation of merchandise
  • Inventory management.
  • Dispatch of foreign trade merchandise
  • Advice on tariff issues
  • Mineral packaging, in super sacks, bags
  • Fertilizer packaging
  • Sector Patterns
  • Sea freight
