What are we?

At Representaciones SOCA Del Pacífico, S.A. de C.V. us We specialize in advising and adequate environmental services, for the correct compliance and Integrated Waste Management according to the Laws, Regulations and Official Mexican Standards to protect the environment environment and human health. We carry out a fundamental work for the sustainable development of industrial activity in Mexico, mainly in Pacific Ports, because we contribute to the Protection of the Environment with a vision of Social Responsibility, we have the corresponding authorizations for handling your waste.

Due to strategic alliances with our partners commercial, we managed to satisfy the needs of our clients, optimizing resources effectively and with the guarantee of offering a fair, professional and safe treatment.


Mission

Perform Comprehensive Waste Management complying with the current official regulations, providing customer peace of mind, ensuring that your waste was disposed of efficiently, transparent and with responsibility.


Vision

Consolidate ourselves as a leading company in the Comprehensive Management of Waste, contributing to the protection of the environment and health human.
