What we offer?

A supply chain supervised by highly specialized personnel in the field of mining, through our team of geologists, chemical engineers, metallurgists and miners, to achieve satisfactory results while taking care of the environment and our operational staff.

To position ourselves as a leading marketer in the minerals market at a global level, providing security and honesty in our commercial relationships with each of our clients. ! !

Offer peace of mind and certainty to our clients, that the minerals, concentrates and metals that we commercialize, comply with the optimal conditions for export, complying with all legal documentation, and supply times for safe marketing.
