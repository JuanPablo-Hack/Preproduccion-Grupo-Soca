Our History

We were born from the vision of creating companies that offer solutions to the needs of customers in the port maritime environment, in the comprehensive management of waste and care for the environment, which has led us to take advantage of the opportunities that the same medium offers us.

Since it is the structure for the development of foreign trade and with the experience of more than 30 years, in innovation in different businesses, it gives us the opportunity to continue with the vision of offering solutions to the needs of foreign trade!


Mission

To be an extension of our clients, guaranteeing legality and trust that gives them peace of mind and satisfaction with results obtained by our business group.


Vision

Position ourselves as a highly trusted group, with processes certificates, which guarantee excellent results.

About us

1

Who are we?

We are a business group originating from the Mexican Pacific coast, consisting of:

  • Arrendadora e Inmobiliaria SOCA S.A. de C.V.
  • Gestión de Materiales y Residuos S.A. de C.V.
  • Representaciones SOCA del Pacifico S.A. de C.V.
  • SOCA Logistics Group S.A. de C.V.
2

What do we do?

We are made up of a human and professional team with more than 20 years of experience focused on:

  • Leasing of transport equipment, machinery and marketing of stone and construction materials.
  • Collection, transport and collection of Hazardous Waste.
  • Collection, transport and collection of solid and urban solid waste Special.
  • Caring for the environment, through comprehensive waste management and environmental advice.
  • Freight, Marketing and Logistic Agent.
