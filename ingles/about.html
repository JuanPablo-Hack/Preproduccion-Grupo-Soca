About

1

What are we?

We are a business group originating from the coasts of the Mexican Pacific, formed by:

  • Arrendadora e Inmobiliaria SOCA S.A. de C.V.
  • Manejo de Materiales y Residuos S.A. de C.V.
  • Representaciones SOCA del Pacifico S.A. de C.V.
  • SOCA Logistics Group S.A. de C.V.
2

How we work?

Which are integrated by a human and professional team with more than 20 years of experience focused on:

  • Leasing of transportation equipment, machinery and marketing of stone and construction materials.
  • Collection, transport and collection of Hazardous Waste.
  • Collection, transport and collection of solid and urban solid waste Special.
  • Caring for the environment, through comprehensive waste management and environmental advice.
  • Cargo, Marketing and Logistics Agent.

Our purpose

  • Providing services of the highest quality.
  • The security of dealing with the client and the offer.
  • The responsibility to provide you with the best experience as our client.
